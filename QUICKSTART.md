# Quick Start Guide

## Your Credit Card Management App is Ready!

The application is now running on: **http://localhost:3000**

## What You Can Do Right Now

### 1. Explore the Dashboard
- View your financial overview with 4 demo credit cards
- Check credit utilization status
- See top spending categories
- View upcoming payment dues

### 2. Manage Cards
- Click on "My Cards" in the sidebar
- Click on any card to see detailed information:
  - Credit limit and available balance
  - Reward rates for different categories
  - Card benefits and perks
  - Payment due dates
- Add a new card using the "Add New Card" button

### 3. Track Expenses & Get Smart Recommendations
- Navigate to "Expenses" section
- Click "Add Expense" button
- Enter merchant name, amount, and category
- **See the Magic**: The app will show you the top 3 recommended cards based on:
  - Maximum rewards for that category
  - Current credit utilization
  - Available credit balance
- Click on the best recommended card and submit

### 4. Analyze Your Spending
- Filter expenses by category or card
- View total spent and rewards earned
- See transaction history with all details
